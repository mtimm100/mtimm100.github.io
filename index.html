    • Hello world, my name is Mikayla.

    I enjoy solving real world problems using machine learning and AI.

    I am currently a Machine Learning Researcher at Systems and Technology Research in Woburn, MA, where I work on a variety of applied research projects involving ML, deep learning, computer vision, and reinforcement learning. I received my Master of Science in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst in January 2020, where I spent my time as a research assistant in the Computer Vision Laboratory advised by Subhransu Maji. I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics from the University of West Florida, where I graduated Summa Cum Laude in spring 2017.

    In my past lives, I've worked as a Machine Learning Research Intern in 2019 at Pinterest in San Francisco, working on the Visual Search Team, and at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) Lincoln Laboratory as a Summer Research Intern working in natural language processing in 2017.

    Aside from being a real life Machine Learning Model (just kidding), I enjoy playing my flute, knitting, taking care of my cat, and going to rock concerts in pre-Covid times.
